Agencies charge $2,000–5,000/month, lock you into contracts, and you don't own anything. BiteBot gives you the same tools — ads, landing pages, lead follow-up, AI receptionist — for $297–497/month with no contracts. You own everything and control your own marketing.

No. The ads, landing pages, and follow-up sequences are all pre-built. You pick an ad, set a budget, and hit launch. The AI handles lead follow-up automatically. We also provide full training and weekly live support calls.
